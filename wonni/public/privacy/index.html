Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 29, 2025

Wonni ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps you list, manage, and cross-post items across online marketplaces. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Wonni, you agree to the practices described here.

1Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (provided when you sign in with Apple)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)
  • Username you set within the app

Listing Content

  • Photos of items you upload
  • Item titles, descriptions, prices, and categories
  • Shipping and condition details

Marketplace Connections

  • OAuth tokens and connected account usernames for third-party platforms (e.g., eBay) that you choose to link

Usage Data

  • App interactions and feature usage (collected via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (collected via Firebase Crashlytics)

2How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store, display, and cross-post your listings to connected marketplaces
  • To authenticate with third-party platforms on your behalf
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To provide customer support

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data to serve you third-party advertising.

3Third-Party Services

Wonni uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase – authentication, database, storage, and analytics
  • eBay Developer Program – marketplace integration (only when you choose to connect your eBay account)
  • Apple Sign In – account authentication
When you connect a marketplace account, we store an OAuth access token on your behalf. We only use this token to perform actions you explicitly initiate within the app (e.g., posting a listing). We never access your marketplace account for any other purpose.

4Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage), which is hosted in the United States. Firebase is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified and encrypts data in transit and at rest.

Photos you upload are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage under access rules that restrict reads to your own account. We apply Firestore Security Rules to ensure users can only read and write their own data.

5Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & correction – You can view and edit your profile information at any time within the app.
  • Disconnect integrations – You can unlink any connected marketplace account from the Settings screen at any time.
  • Delete your account – You may request complete deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us. We will process your request within 30 days.

6Children's Privacy

Wonni is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
